Passenger Trains in CR

Passenger trains in CR can be divided into three types, normal speed trains, high speed trains, and commuter trains. Normal speed trains is powered by locomotives, with low fares attracting people who are not rich. High speed trains, also named Electronic Multiple Units (EMU), have top speed of 250 km/h to 350 km/h. Commuter trains are developed from EMU, but accelerates faster and can hold more passengers.

Normal Speed Trains

25G Rolling Stock

25G is the major type of normal speed carriages in CR. It is air conditioned and can reach a top speed of 120 km/h. It was first developed in 1990s and has been manufactured for over 20 years.

25K Rolling Stock

25K was developed in 1996, having a top speed of 140 km/h. It is mainly applied in express trains. Originally it was in beautiful red and blue paintings, but unfortunately painted green since 2015.

25T Rolling Stock

25T is designed for higher speed and can reach a speed of 160 km/h. It has been the fastest normal speed train in China since 2003. Originally it was in beautiful white and blue paintings, but unfortunately painted green since 2015.

25B Rolling Stock

25B is not air conditioned, so its fare is the cheapest. It serves at local trains in rural area and stops at almost every remote station. Its original color is green with yelloww strip.
